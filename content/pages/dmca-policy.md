## Notification of Claimed Infringement

If you believe that content on this site infringes your copyright, submit a written notification containing:

1. Your physical or electronic signature
2. Description of the copyrighted work(s) claimed to be infringed
3. Description of where the allegedly infringing material is located on the site (including URLs)
4. Your name, address, telephone number, and email address
5. A statement that you have good faith belief the use is unauthorized
6. A statement that the information in the notice is accurate and that you are authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner
7. Your statement under penalty of perjury that you are the copyright owner or authorized representative

Submit to the Copyright Agent listed above.

## Counter-Notice

If you believe your content was wrongly removed, submit a counter-notice containing:

1. Your physical or electronic signature
2. Identification of the removed material and its original location
3. A statement under penalty of perjury that you believe removal was mistaken
4. Your name, address, telephone, and email
5. Statement that you consent to jurisdiction of federal district court and will accept service from the person who submitted the original notice

## Response Procedures

- Upon receipt of valid DMCA notice, we will remove or disable access to allegedly infringing content
- We will notify the content provider of removal
- We will restore content if we receive a valid counter-notice, after 10 business days if original notifier doesn't file suit
- Repeat infringers may have accounts terminated

## Repeat Infringers

We reserve the right to terminate accounts and access for users found to be repeat infringers of copyrights or other intellectual property rights.
