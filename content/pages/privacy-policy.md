# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 12, 2026

Deconstruction Aggregator ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information in accordance with GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable privacy laws.

## 1. Information We Collect

**Automatically Collected:**
- IP address, browser type, operating system
- Pages visited, time spent, links clicked
- Device identifiers and cookie data
- Approximate location (via IP)

**Information You Provide:**
- Email address (newsletter signup, comments, contact forms)
- Username and profile information (user accounts)
- User stories and forum posts (voluntary submission)
- Quiz responses (may be stored anonymously)

**Third-Party Data:**
- Analytics from Google Analytics
- Affiliate conversion data from partners
- Comments via Disqus or similar systems

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- Deliver and improve site services
- Send newsletters (only if opted-in)
- Respond to inquiries
- Analyze usage and optimize content
- Display personalized ads (where applicable)
- Enforce terms and prevent abuse

## 3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We share data with:
- **Supabase** — Database hosting
- **Vercel** — Site hosting and analytics
- **Google Analytics** — Usage analysis
- **Mailchimp** or similar — Email delivery (newsletter)
- **Ad networks** — AdSense, Mediavine, Monumetric, Raptive (anonymized)
- **Affiliate networks** — BetterHelp, Talkspace, Amazon Associates (conversion data)

We do NOT sell personal data. We do not share data with unaffiliated third parties for their marketing purposes.

## 4. Cookies and Tracking

We use:
- **Essential cookies** — Site functionality
- **Analytics cookies** — Google Analytics
- **Advertising cookies** — Ad targeting and measurement
- **Third-party cookies** — Partner services

You can control cookies via browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit functionality.

## 5. Data Retention

- **Website analytics:** 26 months (Google Analytics default)
- **Newsletter subscribers:** Until unsubscribe or 2 years of inactivity
- **User accounts:** Until deletion request
- **Quiz results:** 12 months or until anonymized
- **Forum content:** Until removed by user or moderators

## 6. Your Rights (GDPR, CCPA, Others)

You have the right to:
- **Access** — Request all data we hold about you
- **Correction** — Update inaccurate information
- **Deletion** — Request we delete your data ("Right to be Forgotten")
- **Portability** — Receive your data in portable format
- **Opt-out** — Decline marketing emails and interest-based ads
- **Non-discrimination** — No adverse treatment for exercising rights

**To exercise rights:** Email privacy@deconstruction-aggregator.org with your request and proof of identity.

## 7. Security

We implement:
- SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit
- Secure database access controls
- Regular security audits
- Password hashing and salting

We cannot guarantee absolute security. Use strong passwords and don't share sensitive information.

## 8. Children's Privacy

We comply with COPPA. The site is not intended for users under 13. If we discover a user under 13, we will delete their data. Parents or guardians can contact us to request deletion.

## 9. International Data Transfers

If you're in the EU or UK, your data may be transferred to the US. We comply with Standard Contractual Clauses and GDPR requirements for lawful transfers.

## 10. Do Not Track

Some browsers include "Do Not Track" features. We respond to DNT signals by limiting tracking where feasible, though we may still collect analytics data.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
