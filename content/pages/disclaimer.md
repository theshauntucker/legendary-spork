If you need immediate support:
- **National Suicide Prevention Lifeline:** 988 (call or text)
- **Crisis Text Line:** Text HOME to 741741
- **International Association of Cult Awareness (ICSA):** [icsa.world](https://icsa.world)
- **Cult Recovery Resources:** [cultrecovery.org](https://cultrecovery.org)
- **Trauma-Informed Therapist Finder:** Use your insurance provider or [psychologytoday.com](https://psychologytoday.com)

**Call 1-84-I-DOUBT-IT (1-844-368-3848)** for crisis support from people with cult exit experience.
