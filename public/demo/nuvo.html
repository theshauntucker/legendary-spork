Improvement Priorities — Personalized Training Plan

1
Faster spot on final rotation of the turn sequence
High Impact2–3 Weeks

Training tip: Practice single turns facing a fixed point, holding the spot as long as possible before whipping the head. Layer the full sequence back in only after the single is automatic — the finish should land at full height with zero drift.

2
Sharper attack on musical accents in traveling phrases
High Impact1–2 Months

Training tip: Run the 1:19–1:35 phrase on counts with a hard stop on every accent — “1-2-3-STOP.” Once the stops are clean, restore the flow and keep only the attack. The contrast is what makes lyrical accents land.

3
Sustain shoulder placement out of floor work under fatigue
Medium Impact2–3 Months

Training tip: Add 30-second plank-to-press sequences at the end of rehearsal when tired — placement habits built under fatigue are the ones that hold in the final third of the routine on stage.
